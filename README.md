# msc-thesis-wealth-inequality-italy
Master’s thesis on wealth inequality in Italy, focusing on regional disparities and the role of asset returns using SHIW and National and Regional Accounts data.

# Household Wealth Inequality in Italy: Regional Differences and the Role of Asset Returns

This repository contains the code and materials for my Master's thesis in Economic and Social Sciences at Bocconi University.

## 📌 Overview

This project investigates the distribution of household wealth across Italian macro areas (North, Centre, and South & Islands) and analyzes the role of asset returns in shaping wealth inequality between 2006 and 2022.

The analysis combines microdata from the Survey on Household Income and Wealth (SHIW) with National and Regional Accounts to construct internally consistent wealth distributions. Special attention is given to correcting for top-tail underrepresentation and aligning micro and macro data.

## 🔍 Main Contributions

- Reconstruction of regional wealth distributions in Italy using integrated data sources
- Correction of survey biases through Pareto tail adjustments using external rich lists
- Analysis of portfolio composition across wealth groups and macro areas
- Construction of asset-specific return series for real and financial assets
- Counterfactual simulation to isolate the role of asset returns in wealth dynamics

## 📊 Key Findings

- Wealth inequality increased over the period, particularly in the North
- The Top 1% holds a significantly larger share of wealth compared to the Bottom 50%
- Portfolio composition differs substantially across wealth groups:
  - Wealthier households are more exposed to financial assets
  - Lower wealth groups rely heavily on housing and hold higher liabilities
- Average portfolio returns increase along the wealth distribution
- Counterfactual simulations show that asset returns alone explain a large share of wealth dynamics:
  - Up to ~85% of top wealth variation in the North

## 🧠 Methodology

The empirical strategy is based on:

- Micro-macro reconciliation (SHIW + National/Regional Accounts)
- Pareto tail correction for top wealth
- Construction of real asset and financial asset return series
- Counterfactual portfolio simulation with fixed portfolio structures
- Regression analysis of observed vs simulated wealth dynamics
